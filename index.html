Concert Masterlist

An application designed to help music enthusiasts and casual concert goers keep track of past events and the memories attached.

Enter the date, venue, performers, pictures, and comments about the experience in an easy to read, easy to interact environment. Search by date, artist, or venue. You will always have a complete record of your concert adventures.

In the detailed view of a specific concert, a Spotify player is loaded for each band so you can relive that show. Forget who a band is? Play a few songs from the player to jog your memory. Never forget a concert again.

Python Snake Game Python Speech to Text
Experience the classic Snake Game with a modern twist. Developed in Python, this project extends beyond a tutorial by incorporating features like the ability to replay after a game over with a simple press of the space bar, and tracking the high score throughout the session. It's a blend of nostalgia and innovation, demonstrating my ability to enhance basic concepts with user-focused features. Whether you're revisiting a childhood favorite or appreciating the nuances of Python programming, this Snake Game offers a compelling mix of challenge and charm. This project is a straightforward tool developed in Python for converting audio recordings into text. Using the computer's microphone, it records audio, then sends the recording to AssemblyAI for transcription. The resulting text file is saved directly to the computer. It's a compact project that emerged from following a tutorial, which I enhanced to fit my needs for potential future projects.
