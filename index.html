Concert Masterlist

An application designed to help music enthusiasts and casual concert goers keep track of past events and the memories attached.

Enter the date, venue, performers, pictures, and comments about the experience in an easy to read, easy to interact environment. Search by date, artist, or venue. You will always have a complete record of your concert adventures.

In the detailed view of a specific concert, a Spotify player is loaded for each band so you can relive that show. Forget who a band is? Play a few songs from the player to jog your memory. Never forget a concert again.

Movie Collection

Keep track of the movies you own

Add movies to your collection with an easy to use app. Enter all of the details and it will be added to your collection. If you click on a poster, it takes you to the information page for that movie. That view includes a description and the genres associated with the movie.
